The Trader's Toolkit: An Order Type Explorer

Every order type represents a strategic choice, balancing speed, price certainty, and risk. Select an order from the list to understand its unique mechanics and trade-offs.

    Market Order

    Buy or sell immediately at the best available price. Offers the highest chance of execution but no price guarantee.

    Primary Use Case:

    Urgent entry or exit, especially in highly liquid markets.

    Primary Risk:

    Price Slippage. The execution price may differ from the last-seen price, especially in volatile markets.

    Order Lifecycle & Time in Force

    An order is a dynamic entity that moves through a defined lifecycle. Hover over the states below to see how an order progresses from submission to completion.

    Order State Machine

    1

    Pending

    Submitted but not yet active (e.g., after hours).
    2

    Working

    Live order sent to the market, awaiting execution.
    3

    Partially Filled

    Part of the order is executed; the rest is still working.
    4

    Filled

    The order has been completely executed.
    5

    Canceled

    Order proactively canceled by the user before a full fill.
